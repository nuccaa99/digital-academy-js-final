A LIVING HISTORY

Coffee point is the Coffee House and Restaurant at The Trailhead. A Family-owned business that blends our family's culture and foods from Argentina, Armenia and Italy. Serving a healthy, high quality menu, with an emphasis on incorporating locally sourced ingredients and products. Serving several Buenos Aires style coffee drinks, as well as mate, a traditional Argentine tea. We offer different styles of Empanadas with a chimichurri sauce, along with many other cultural staple foods.

Our home

Located at the edge of downtown, the Trailhead Compound embodies human centered design. Our building is an example of adaptive reuse principles, which seeks to re-purpose old architecture for new multi-use purposes while maintaining historic preservation and conserving land. The transparent dining space retains elements of the historic building and is an environment connecting with the healing gardens that surround the building, providing a fresh and rejuvenating experience.
